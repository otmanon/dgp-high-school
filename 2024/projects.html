Heat Me Baby One More Time

Hot-summer nights, a cool breeze in the air, ice-cold coke in your hand... what could be better? These everyday temperature-driven experiences are all governed by the concept of diffusion. This project will teach you all about how diffusion works, and have you code up your own simulation of a diffusion process!

Keyframe Animation

How do movies make all those fake digital characters move? Some walk and move like humans, others crawl and move like bugs... but no matter the motion, it all boils down to one fundamental practice; keyframe animation! This project will introduce you to some of the mathematical tools used behind-the-scenes in animation tools used by artists to this day!

Low-Poly Fabrication

Transform imagination into reality with fabrication techniques, turning 2D images and 3D shapes into stylized, coarser versions for laser cutting and 3D printing. Learn and apply common shape simplification techniques to craft your designs, then see them come to life with our fabrication tools!

Numerical Simulation of Planetary Orbit

Numerical Simulation of Planetary Orbits One of the most well-known physical phenomena is gravitational orbits, or how celestial bodies can revolve around other, larger celestial bodies. Being able to simulate these is an important building block for simulating other, more complex astrophysical systems like our solar system. This project will introduce you to how simulation works on a computer, some basic methods for estimating the motion of these orbiting bodies, and how these deceptively simple methods can produce incredibly accurate (or sometimes inaccurate!) results.

Voronoi Puzzles

Discover the significance of Voronoi diagrams in nature, where they play a crucial role in elegantly representing spatial relationships and optimizing resource distribution! Dive into the intricacies of Voronoi diagrams as you learn how to construct them. Utilizing this newfound knowledge, you'll embark on the exciting journey of crafting your unique Voronoi puzzle through laser cutting, a masterpiece to proudly bring back home and showcase your creativity.

Boyds

Dive into an exciting high school project on crowd simulation and emergent behaviors inspired by Boyd's principles! Explore the world of computational modeling, discover how individual decisions shape group dynamics, and get hands-on with crowd simulation algorithms. This blend of computer science and behavioral science promises a fun journey to unleash your creativity and analytical skills in studying collective behaviors!
